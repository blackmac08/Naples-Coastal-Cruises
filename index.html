Where We Cruise

Serving Naples & Bonita Springs with a focus on calm-water, backwater, and estuary sightseeing.

Common areas:

Cocohatchee River backwaters • Little Hickory Bay • Wiggins Pass & nearby waterways • Delnor-Wiggins area • local canals and mangrove shorelines (conditions permitting)

The Boat

A stable, spacious covered pontoon tour boat designed for comfortable sightseeing. Enjoy shaded seating, Bluetooth audio, a cooler with ice, and easy boarding.

We carry up to 6 passengers in compliance with USCG passenger-for-hire limits for this operation.

What’s Included

• Cooler with ice • Bottled water • Bluetooth music • USCG-required safety gear

Bring sun protection, snacks/drinks, beachwear, bug spray, and a light layer for evenings.
